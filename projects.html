Computer Vision Robot Hand!

When I first entered college, I was introduced to a senior who had snuck a 3D printer into his dormitory room. We hit it off and he taught me lots about 3D Printing, and before long, I was dreaming up ideas about what I could make. Of course, i chose to make a cool robot hand, because why the heck not, right?


The robot fingers move with the help of four (really strong!) servo motors, all controlled with an Arduino Uno. The joints are made of flexible TPU while the shell is made of PLA.

After assembling the hand and testing the movement with basic arduino code, I decided to upgrade the robot hand with computer vision capabilities, and make it replicate my hand gestures!
